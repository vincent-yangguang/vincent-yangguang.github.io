Guang Yang (杨光)

Chief Architect / Research Scientist
XG Lab at Alibaba DAMO Academy

Email: helu.yg (at) alibaba-inc.com

Yang Guang got his PHD degree from Beijing University of Post and Telecommunications since 2006. He used to be Director of wireless department of China Mobile Research Institute from 2016 and chief Architect of XG lab of Alibaba DAMO academy since 2020. Yang Guang got his Professor-level senior engineer since 2015.

He takes responsibility of 4G (TD-LTE) technique research, standard, lab and field tail, and his project “4G (TD-LTE) technology and application” wins the Special-class award of National Science and Technology Progress Award 2016. He currently led the cloud-native mobile network (5G) research in Alibaba DAMO academy.
